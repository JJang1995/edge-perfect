How It Works

Simple Process, Perfect Results

Getting your knives sharpened has never been easier.

01

Drop Off or We Pick Up

Bring your knives to us, or we'll come to your location. Whatever is most convenient for you.

02

Inspection

We assess each blade, identify damage, and select the right sharpening approach.

03

Sharpening

Hand-sharpened on premium whetstones. Each blade finished and honed to a razor edge.

04

Return

Pick up your knives from us or we'll drop them back off at your door. Razor sharp and ready to use.
